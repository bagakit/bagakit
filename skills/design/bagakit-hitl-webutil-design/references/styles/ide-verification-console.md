# IDE Verification Console

## Route

- Fixed shell with independent pane scrolling.
- Toolbar or icon action treatment, low chrome, low corner radius, high density.

## Good For

- Manual execution, inspection, and one-case-at-a-time verification.

## Control Treatment

- Left inventory, center procedure, right result or export is the default route.
- Keep live status close to the active case.

## Failure Signals

- The page reads like a marketing dashboard.
- Pane density hides the active step or result state.
