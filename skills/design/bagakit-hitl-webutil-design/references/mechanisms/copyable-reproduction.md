# Copyable Reproduction

## Owns

- Provide copyable commands, scripts, or setup blocks that reduce ambiguity.
- Keep launch or reproduction inputs near the task that needs them.

## Does Not Own

- Terminal aesthetics or scene-wide density treatment.

## Design Checks

- Copying one block is enough to start the right environment or reproduction.
- Inputs, placeholders, and prerequisites are visible.

## Outputs Or Evidence

- Copyable setup text with execution context.

## Failure Signals

- The human must rewrite commands manually.
- Setup depends on hidden context not surfaced near the block.
