# Case Inventory

## Owns

- Present the work items, cases, or verification units the human must browse.
- Support grouping, priority, and scan order.

## Does Not Own

- Per-case instructions or report typography.

## Design Checks

- The human can tell what to work on next.
- Inventory state is scannable without opening every item.

## Outputs Or Evidence

- Visible ordering, grouping, and per-case state.

## Failure Signals

- The list is only a dump of labels.
- The human cannot tell case scope or priority at a glance.
