# Knowledge Transfer

## Owns

- Structure the page so the human can absorb and reconstruct the key model.
- Use chunking, anchors, examples, and media only when they improve recall.

## Does Not Own

- Overall page skin, pane layout, or scene-specific chrome.

## Design Checks

- Core claims are grouped and sequenced.
- The page exposes what matters now versus supporting detail.

## Outputs Or Evidence

- A restatable model, checklist, or summary packet the human can return.

## Failure Signals

- The page is dense but not learnable.
- Key ideas are present yet hard to reconstruct from memory.
